BalochiResources is a free, open, community-maintained resource platform for Baloch, Balochi, and Balochistan.

Whether you're a learner, a linguist, a researcher, a member of the diaspora, or simply curious — this site is your starting point.

## How to contribute

All resources live as Markdown files in this repository. To add or update a resource, open a pull request on GitHub.

## Categories

- **[Dictionaries & Lexicons](/dictionaries/)** — Balochi dictionaries, glossaries, and lexicons — in print, PDF, web, and app form.
- **[Language Learning & Grammar](/learning/)** — Grammars, textbooks, handbooks, and structured resources for learning Balochi.
- **[Literature & Culture](/literature/)** — Balochi poetry, prose, folklore, oral traditions, and cultural studies.
- **[Academic & Linguistics](/academic/)** — Peer-reviewed papers and scholarly research on Balochi linguistics.
- **[Culture & Media](/media/)** — Audio recordings, video lectures, and multimedia resources.
- **[Digital Libraries & Archives](/libraries/)** — Online collections and archives hosting large bodies of Balochi material.
- **[Tools & Technology](/tools/)** — Apps, keyboards, input tools, and software for the Balochi language.
- **[Religion & Manuscripts](/religion/)** — Religious texts, translations, and manuscript resources in Balochi.
- **[Community & Organizations](/community/)** — Institutions, academies, and organizations working with the Balochi language.
